What exactly is the Replica?

It’s a productized decision engine that mirrors Mo’s reasoning patterns with operational safeguards. It turns any messy brief into a short, safe plan you can execute this week.

1) Lenses

Iceberg: separate surface signals from deeper causes.
POL: convert opposites (win/loss, price up/down) into opportunities at S1/S2/S3 depths.

2) Gates

Decisions pass Evidence, Risk, Ethics, and Trust gates. If a gate fails, delivery pauses—no guesswork.

3) Harness

A “golden set” of cases to calibrate the replica. We track Replica-Match% and only ship when it meets the acceptance bar.

How it works (14-Day Sprint)

Short, measurable, and reversible. Designed to move one number safely.

Day 0–1

  • 30-min intake (7 quick questions).
  • Plain-English Judgment + 3 steps + early KPI.

Days 2–13

  • Light follow-ups (WhatsApp/email), small tweaks.
  • If KPI moves → prepare S2 (structural).
  • If KPI stalls → trigger the Kill-Switch.

Result: a Before/After snapshot with 1–2 KPIs, plus a minimal SOP to keep the gain.
