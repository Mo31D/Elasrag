Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need special tools?

No, we work with the tools you have and help you choose the right digital tools if needed.

How are results measured?

Using clear KPIs such as EHR, cycle time, and number of deliveries or sales.

Is this legal or tax advice?

No, we provide organizational and strategic consultancy only. Please consult legal/tax professionals for that.

What do “one-way” and “two-way” mean?

A one-way door decision is irreversible and executed quickly; a two-way door decision is reversible and requires additional review.
